Pesto Pasta with Chicken

Description

This chicken pesto pasta is easy and delicious. Serve with crusty bread and salad for a quick dinner. Use as much or as little pesto sauce as you like. Using homemade pesto will taste even better, but it adds to prep time. Enjoy!

This chicken pesto pasta is a quick dinner for the days you don't feel like cooking, but still enjoy eating something delicious. If you've had a long day, or maybe you're sick, a simple dinner will ease your mind and your stomach. Serve with bread or salad to complete the meal.

This recipe is easy to modify with different ingredients. Use less or more pesto sauce depending on how much you like it, or change up the vegetables in the dish. This recipe could even be used as a starting point for an entirely new dish.

Ingredients

  • 16oz package bowtie pasta
  • 1tsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/2c pesto sauce
  • 1/3c oil-packed sundried tomatoes

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saut√© garlic until tender, then stir in chicken and season with red pepper flakes. Cook until chicken is golden and cooked through.
  3. Combine pasta, chicken, pesto, and sun-dried tomatoes in a large bowl; toss to coat evenly.
