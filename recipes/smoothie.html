PB&J Smoothie

Description

The pb&j smoothie is a yummy, delicious, nutritious, easy to make, quick to prepare, and little-to-no cleanup beverage that most everyone enjoys. This drink combines the breakfast classic pb&j (peanut butter and jelly) with the smoothie. The smoothie gives it a creamy texture while the jelly gives it an added flavor.

To make the pb&j smoothie, throw all the ingredients into the blender and blendâ€”that's it! You can even use frozen bananas if you want it really cold. It's that easy!

Ingredients

  • 6 frozen strawberries
  • 3/4c milk
  • 1 1/2tbsp peanut butter
  • 1tbsp blue agave nectar
  • 1tbsp flax seeds

Directions

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, peanut butter, blue agave, and flax seeds in a blender and blend until well combined.
